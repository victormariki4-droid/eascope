Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the best time to visit East Africa for a safari?

East Africa has excellent safari year-round! The dry seasons (June–October and January–February) are generally considered best for game viewing. The Great Migration river crossings in Masai Mara and Serengeti happen July–October. Gorilla trekking is great year-round but June–September and December–February tend to have drier trail conditions.

Are gorilla permits included in your packages?

Yes — all gorilla permits are included in our Uganda and Rwanda gorilla trekking packages. Permits are limited and often sell out months in advance, so we strongly recommend booking at least 3–6 months ahead to secure your preferred dates.

What is included in your safari packages?

All packages include: 4×4 safari vehicle with pop-up roof, experienced professional guide, accommodation, all park and game reserve fees, park permits, airport transfers, and most meals. International flights, travel insurance, visa fees, and personal expenses are not included. We will always be transparent about exactly what is included.

Can you create a fully custom itinerary?

Absolutely — this is our specialty! Every Eascope itinerary can be fully tailored to your travel dates, group size, budget, accommodation preference, and specific safari interests. Simply contact us with your requirements and we'll build your dream African journey from scratch.

How do I book and what is the payment process?

Simply fill in the enquiry form above or WhatsApp us directly. We will respond within 24 hours with a detailed proposal. A 30% deposit secures your booking, with the balance due 60 days before departure. We accept bank transfers, PayPal, and major credit cards.
