About Rwanda

The Land of a Thousand Hills

Rwanda is one of Africa's greatest transformations — a nation reborn, clean, safe, and strikingly beautiful. Rolling verdant hills, mist-shrouded volcanoes sheltering mountain gorillas, ancient chimpanzee forests, serene Lake Kivu, and Kigali — Africa's most orderly and vibrant capital. Rwanda offers an intimate, high-end safari experience like nowhere else on the continent.

Top Attractions

Rwanda's Best Destinations

From gorilla families in the volcanoes to the crystal shores of Lake Kivu.

National Park

Volcanoes National Park

Home to the famous gorilla families first studied by Dian Fossey. Trek through bamboo forests and ancient volcanoes for an intimate hour with mountain gorilla families — one of Earth's most profound wildlife encounters.

Discover More
National Park

Nyungwe Forest National Park

One of Africa's oldest and most biodiverse rainforests. Walk the famous Canopy Walkway 70m above the forest floor, track chimpanzees, colobus monkeys, and over 310 bird species.

Discover More
Lake & Leisure

Lake Kivu

One of Africa's Great Lakes — serene blue waters framed by rolling green hills. Relax at luxury lakeside lodges in Gisenyi or Kibuye, kayak to island fishing villages, and sample fresh fish at sunset.

Discover More
City & Culture

Kigali City Tours

Africa's cleanest and fastest-growing capital. Visit the Genocide Memorial, the vibrant Kimironko Market, the Inema Arts Centre, and enjoy the city's world-class dining scene.

Discover More
National Park

Akagera National Park

Rwanda's savanna safari gem — recently restocked with lions and rhinos, Akagera offers classic Big Five game drives through papyrus-fringed lakes and open grasslands in the eastern lowlands.

Discover More
Wildlife

Golden Monkey Trekking

Found only in the Virunga Volcanoes — track the playful and rare Golden monkey through bamboo forests of Volcanoes National Park. A delightful and often hilarious primate encounter.
