Kigali City Tours

Africa's cleanest, safest capital — a moving story of rebirth told through memorials, markets, art studios and exceptional coffee.

Highlight

A Story of Rebirth

Begin at the Kigali Genocide Memorial — a profoundly moving, beautifully curated space that honours the past and explains the country's extraordinary journey since 1994.

Then see the result on every street: spotless boulevards, flowering roundabouts, and a calm, confident city that reinvented itself within a generation.

Highlight

Markets, Makers & Coffee

Walk the lively Nyamirambo quarter with local women guides, bargain for imigongo art and fabrics at Kimironko market, and meet the makers in Kigali's thriving studio scene.

Between stops, drink some of the best coffee on the planet in cafés that would hold their own in any capital on Earth.
