City & Day Tours · Kenya

Nairobi City Tours

The only capital on Earth with a national park on its doorstep — lions against a skyline, baby elephants at breakfast and giraffes at eye level.

Highlight

Safari Before Lunch

Nairobi National Park delivers the world's most surreal game drive: rhinos, lions, buffalo and giraffe grazing against a backdrop of city skyscrapers, just twenty minutes from your hotel.

Pair it with the Sheldrick Wildlife Trust's morning mud-bath, where orphaned baby elephants tumble over their keepers — one of the most heart-melting hours in Africa.

Plan This Experience
Highlight

Giraffes, History & Flavours

At the Giraffe Centre, endangered Rothschild's giraffes lean down to greet you eye to eye. Next door, the Karen Blixen Museum keeps the 'Out of Africa' story alive at the foot of the Ngong Hills.

Finish with Nairobi's famous food scene — from carnivore grills to leafy garden cafés — and a browse through vibrant Maasai craft markets.
