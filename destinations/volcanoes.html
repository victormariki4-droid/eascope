Gorilla Trekking · Rwanda

Volcanoes National Park

Dian Fossey's misty volcanoes — the world's most accessible mountain gorilla trekking, wrapped in bamboo forest and Rwandan hospitality.

Highlight

Gorillas in the Mist — For Real

This is the forest Dian Fossey made famous. A dozen habituated gorilla families live on these bamboo-clad volcano slopes, and treks here are the most accessible in Africa — some families barely an hour's walk from the trailhead.

When the silverback fixes you with his calm, intelligent gaze, cameras drop and jaws follow. Nothing prepares you for it.

Plan This Experience
Highlight

Beyond the Gorillas

Hike to Dian Fossey's research camp and grave at Karisoke, climb Bisoke's crater lake, or trek the bamboo in search of golden monkeys — playful, copper-coated primates found only in these mountains.

Around the park, Rwanda itself shines: immaculate villages, terraced emerald hills and the warmest of welcomes.
