Our Story

From a Dream to
1,200+ Happy Travelers

Eascope Expeditions was founded with a single, powerful conviction: that encountering Africa's wild places should be transformative, responsible, and accessible to all who seek it.

Rooted in Arusha — the safari capital of Tanzania — our team of passionate local experts has spent over a decade perfecting the art of the African safari. We combine insider knowledge with genuine care for every traveler and deep respect for the wildlife and communities we work alongside.

Today, we are proud to be one of East Africa's most trusted safari companies — with an unbroken streak of 5-star reviews and a growing community of repeat travelers who return year after year because, quite simply, Africa gets into your soul.

What We Believe

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every safari we run and every relationship we build.

🌍

Authentic Africa

We believe in real, unfiltered encounters with Africa — not manufactured spectacles. We go to where the wildlife is, guided by decades of local knowledge.

💚

Conservation First

A portion of every safari supports local conservation projects and anti-poaching initiatives. We protect the Africa that makes our work possible.

🤝

Community Partnership

We employ and support local guides, lodges, and artisans — ensuring that tourism benefits the communities who share their home with wildlife.

✨

Tailored Perfection

No two safaris are identical. Every itinerary is hand-built around your passions, travel style, and budget. We craft experiences, not itineraries.

🛡️

Safety & Trust

Fully licensed, insured, and regulated. Our vehicles are immaculately maintained and our guides are first-aid certified and wildlife emergency trained.

🏆

Excellence Always

From the first enquiry to the farewell at the airport, our standard is unwavering excellence — in service, in logistics, in the moments we create.
